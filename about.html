welcome to amco steels

At Amco steels, we offer a variety of Iron and Steel products ranging from mild steel structurals to plates. Our product range includes M.S Angles, M.S Channels, M.S Beams, M.S Plates, Hot Rolled Sheets, Cold Rolled Sheets, etc. of various dimensions and thickness. The products are sourced from major Steel manufactures like Steel Authority of India Limited, Essar Steel, Jindal Steel, Lloyd Steel and other ISI steel manufacturers from different parts of the country Amco Steel is run in proprietorship of Mr Abdhul rasack, a man with more than two decades of experience in the Iron and Steel trade. The rich experience and hard work of the promoter has made the Company a leader in the Steel trade Our service and product quality standard have won us accolades from a variety of esteem customers, which includes major infrastructure companies, builders and architects.We at Amco Steels believe in building relationship and the testimony to this is that our customers continue to remain with us.
